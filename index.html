    Experience

    // research & industry

    Cheminformatics Intern

    Merck Research Laboratories, Cambridge

    June 2025 – August 2025

    • Built a data pipeline to identify functionally relevant proteins to a target of interest using protein families, domains, sequence homology, and interaction mechanisms.
    • Drew on Merck's chemical matter knowledge and external databases to recommend tool compounds to the Discovery Chemistry team.

    Undergraduate Researcher + Teaching Assistant

    Big Data in Biology Research Lab, UT Austin

    January 2023 – Present

    • Examined gene expression differences across brain regions in binge-drinking–predisposed mice using bulk RNA-seq (R, Python).
    • Developed ML models to distinguish genetic lines based on binge-drinking–associated expression profiles.
    • Mentored 6 students on a spatial transcriptomics snRNA-seq project investigating cell type differences in Bipolar Disorder vs. Control human samples.
    • Hosted Python workshops and open houses; graded coding assignments for 25 students.
    • Earned 2023 Summer Fellowship Research Funding (1 of 4 recipients).

    Undergraduate Research Assistant

    Fonken Lab, UT Austin

    November 2023 – Present

    • Investigated how Mycobacterium vaccae immunization protects against neuroinflammation and cognitive decline in aged male mice using snRNA-seq (hdWGCNA, CellChat).
    • Examined communication differences in offspring of maternally immune-activated dams via ultrasonic vocalization analysis (MATLAB – DeepSqueak, VocalMat).
    • Explored how a two-hit stressor model alters microglial morphology in the hippocampus and prefrontal cortex using digital tracing in Imaris.
    • Investigating virome and microbiome composition differences between ASD and control siblings using bulk RNA-seq.

    Undergraduate Research Assistant

    Snodderly Lab, UT Austin

    August 2022 – June 2023

    • Investigated how sex-linked polymorphic color vision shapes foraging behavior in New World monkeys using Support Vector Machine modeling.
    • Cleaned and organized 7 years of field data (2015–2022) to streamline integration with modeling scripts.
    • Developed a standardized documentation system to improve collaboration between field ecologists and data scientists.

    Machine Learning Interpretability Team Member

    Longhorn NeuroTech Club, UT Austin

    August 2024 – June 2025

    • Evaluated models processing EEG signals using CNNs and Capsule Networks.
    • Applied the Captum package (integrated gradients, feature ablation) to improve model interpretability and performance.
